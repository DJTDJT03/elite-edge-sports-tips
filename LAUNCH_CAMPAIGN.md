## Crisis Management

### If we have a bad losing streak:
- Post every result transparently
- Thread: "5 losses in a row — here's what happened and what we're adjusting"
- Never delete losing tips
- Show long-term P/L graph to contextualise
- Reduce tip volume until edge returns

### If someone accuses us of fake results:
- Link to timestamped, publicly published results page
- Show the full record including losses
- Don't engage in arguments — let the data speak

### If a competitor copies our content:
- Screenshot and timestamp everything
- DMCA takedown if they copy analysis verbatim
- IP protection is in the T&Cs
